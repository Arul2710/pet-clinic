Emergency Information

In case of a life-threatening emergency, please call our emergency hotline immediately or visit our clinic directly. Our emergency team is staffed 24/7 with experienced veterinarians and support personnel ready to provide immediate care. Common emergencies include difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, poisoning, seizures, traumatic injuries, and difficulty giving birth.
