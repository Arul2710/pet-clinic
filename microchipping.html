Simple Process

How Microchipping Works

Quick, painless, and over before you know it — here is the simple process we follow.

1

Consultation

We discuss your pet's health history and answer any questions you have about the procedure.

2

Quick Implant

The microchip is injected between the shoulder blades — it takes just 2-3 seconds, like a routine vaccination.

3

Scan & Verify

We scan the chip to confirm it's reading correctly and record the unique ID number in your pet's file.

4

Registration

We register your pet's chip in a national database with your contact info — active for life.

The entire appointment takes about 10-15 minutes from start to finish.

Peace of Mind

Is Microchipping Safe?

Absolutely. Microchipping is one of the safest veterinary procedures performed worldwide.

FDA-Approved & Tested

Microchips are made from biocompatible materials and have been rigorously tested for safety. They are FDA-approved for use in animals.

Quick & Minimally Invasive

The chip is pre-loaded in a sterile needle and injected under the skin. The procedure takes seconds and most pets barely notice.

No Side Effects

Serious complications are extremely rare ” far less common than with most other veterinary procedures. No batteries, no moving parts, nothing to maintain.

Privacy Protected

The chip itself contains only a unique ID number. Your personal contact information is stored securely in the database ” never on the chip.
